Validation lanes:

- `analytic`: closed-form law, sign convention, monotonicity, or exact diagonalisation target
- `literature`: paper-backed reproduction, review-backed interpretation, or existing `test_against_papers` workflow
- `workflow`: exact-vs-sampled, replay, fidelity, or internal consistency check rather than a theory-overlay claim

Status labels:

- `pass`: the checked-in asset matches the documented claim and has a declared validation target
- `fixed 2026-04-21`: repaired during this audit pass
